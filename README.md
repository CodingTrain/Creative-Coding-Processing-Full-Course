# Creative Coding with Processing-4
Welcome aboard to the world of creative coding! Join me in this beginner-friendly video series as we embark on an exciting adventure with Processing, a versatile and artist-friendly programming language and environment. Whether you're an absolute beginner or a seasoned coder, my goal is to inspire and empower you to unleash your creativity through coding. Processing is your gateway to making art, animations, and interactive experiences. No prior experience is requiredâ€”just curiosity and a desire to create!
